About 10 years ago, I received a pink panther charm as a gift. I liked it very much, so I started to learn how to make it. Through lessons and books, I've managed to do it. In the beginning, it was an overwhelming experience. There was a lot of information to go through. Now looking back, I would suggest for newcomers to go with the simplest project at first. No worry. The rest of the skills will come. You will get better and better with each new project.

Thanks for looking!
